# 🏋️ JYM PRO - User Guide

## Welcome to JYM Pro Fitness App

A professional, feature-rich fitness tracking application with an elegant neon green UI/UX design.

## 🚀 Getting Started

### Splash Screen
- App launches with animated intro
- Displays "JYM PRO" branding and "TRAIN • TRACK • TRANSFORM" tagline
- Automatically transitions to main dashboard

## 📱 Main Navigation

### Bottom Navigation Bar
Located at the bottom with 4 main sections:

1. **HOME** 🏠
   - Daily progress overview
   - Progress ring showing achievement
   - Calorie, heart points, and active minutes tracking
   - Quick access to metrics: Steps, Heart Rate, BMI, Calories
   - Favorites workout section
   - Hamburger menu for quick navigation

2. **WEIGHT** ⚖️
   - Log your weight entries
   - View weight history with chart
   - Track BMI trends
   - Body fat percentage
   - Weight goal setting
   - Historical data visualization

3. **COMMUNITY** 👥
   - Join fitness clubs and groups
   - See trending communities
   - View community feed
   - Connect with other fitness enthusiasts
   - Community challenges and events

4. **PROFILE** 👤
   - Update personal information
   - Set fitness goals (Lose, Maintain, Gain)
   - Track height and body metrics
   - Manage profile settings
   - Save fitness preferences

## 💾 Data Management

### Profile Setup
1. Go to **PROFILE** tab
2. Enter your name
3. Set your height (cm)
4. Select your sex
5. Choose your primary goal
6. Tap **SAVE PROFILE**

### Weight Tracking
1. Go to **WEIGHT** tab
2. Tap **LOG WEIGHT**
3. Enter your weight value
4. Date/time auto-populated
5. Tap **SAVE WEIGHT**
6. View history and trends

### Fitness Goals
- **Lose**: Fat loss focus with cardio recommendations
- **Maintain**: Balanced approach with mixed training
- **Gain**: Muscle building with strength focus

## 📊 Metrics Explained

### BMI (Body Mass Index)
- Calculated from weight and height
- Shows: Healthy / Elevated / High
- Updated when you log weight

### Steps
- Daily step count
- Helps track daily activity
- Works with device sensors

### Heart Rate
- BPM (Beats Per Minute)
- Indicates cardiovascular health
- Updated from device sensors

### Calories
- Daily calorie burn
- Remaining calories for the day
- Goal-based calculation

### Active Minutes
- Time spent in physical activity
- Contributes to daily goal
- Tracked automatically

## 🚨 Troubleshooting

### App Won't Start
- Ensure Flutter is updated: `flutter upgrade`
- Clear cache: `flutter clean`
- Reinstall dependencies: `flutter pub get`

### Data Not Saving
- Check if you tapped SAVE button
- Verify all required fields are filled
- Check device storage availability

### UI Issues
- Restart the app
- Force close and reopen
- Clear app cache from device settings

## 🎓 Understanding Recommendations

The app provides personalized workout suggestions based on:
- Your fitness goal (Lose/Maintain/Gain)
- Your BMI category
- Your height and weight

### Goal-Specific Recommendations

**Lose Weight:**
- Fat Loss Split (4-6 days/week)
- Morning Cardio (30-40 min)
- HIIT Training (15-20 min)

**Maintain:**
- Balanced Weekly Plan (4-5 days/week)
- Mobility & Flexibility (20-30 min)
- Endurance Training (45-60 min)

**Gain Muscle:**
- Muscle Gain Split (4-5 days/week)
- Heavy Compounds (60-75 min)
- Recovery Sessions (20-30 min)
